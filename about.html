About Us

At ReVIVE, we are dedicated to creating eco-friendly solutions by transforming unused textiles into valuable products. We work with tailors, cloth vendors, and small shopkeepers to promote sustainability through reusable cloth bags.

Our mission is to Relive, Reuse, and Recycle by connecting communities and reducing waste. With our platform, vendors can sell surplus cloth, and tailors can craft eco-friendly bags that shopkeepers can purchase.

How We Do It

We bring together vendors with surplus cloth, tailors skilled in crafting reusable bags, and shopkeepers looking for sustainable options. Through our platform:

  • Vendors list unused materials at affordable rates.
  • Tailors create custom-sized or standard reusable bags.
  • Shopkeepers browse and purchase eco-friendly bags, reducing plastic use.
